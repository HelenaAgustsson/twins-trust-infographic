Twins Trust Centre for Research and Excellence 2020/21

More than 1500 ultrasound scans carried out.

Almost 200 twin & triplet babies were welcomed into the world.
We published 7 research papers to help inform future treatment and care for our families.

1,070 doctors, midwives and consultants attended our 11 webinars on caring for expectant multiples birth families.
We recorded 472 pregnancies in the Multiple Birth Registry, which helps improve the care of women with a twin or triplet pregnancy and their babies.
